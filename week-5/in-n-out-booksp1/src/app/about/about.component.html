Truman Capote

Truman Capote (1924-1984) was well known novelist that transitioned from being a short story writer to a more journalistic novel style writer. Mr. Capote published a popular novel titled Breakfast at Tiffany’s in 1958 and a movie came out of that three years later. After much success, later in 1965, he published another big time novel titled In Cold Blood. That novel got so much attention that it was brought to the big screen just two years after publication in 1967.
