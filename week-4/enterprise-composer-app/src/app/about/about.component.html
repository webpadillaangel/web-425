Franz Liszt

Portrait of Franz Liszt by Friedrich von Amerling

A Hungarian composer whom also played the piano, taught music, and the played the organ. Not only did he compose music and play multiple instruments, he was a writer as well. Franz was born on October 22, 1811 in Hungary. An awesome fact about Franz is that he wrote more than 700 compositions! He started composing music at a young age which paved the way to his success. He had a difficult last ten years of his life and his life came to an end on July 31, 1886 in Germany.
